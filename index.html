About Me

I am a Computer Science student focused on building practical projects while strengthening my foundations in Python and cloud computing. I learn best by building real systems and understanding how things work end-to-end, from core logic and workflows to deployment and maintenance.

I have built and deployed Ordixa, a role-based order management platform designed to handle multi-user workflows with clear role separation. Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in backend logic, access control, authentication, debugging real production issues, and maintaining a live application.

My interest in AI comes from its potential to augment human capabilities and introduce intelligence into software systems, while cloud computing enables real-world deployment, scalability, and reliability of applications. I see both domains as complementary and essential for building modern software products.

At this stage, my goal is to build strong technical fundamentals, develop disciplined learning habits, and gradually move toward building intelligent, cloud-based applications through well-designed projects.
